brooklyn style pizza

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Dough:

  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups bread flout
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

Toppings:

  • 6 oz. low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn

Steps:

  1. Make dough and let sit
  2. Preheat oven at 550 F (288 C)
  3. Stretch dough and add topings
  4. Place in preheated oven to brown crust ~4-6 min
  5. Remove pizza and add basil leaves
